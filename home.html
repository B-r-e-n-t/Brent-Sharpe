About Me

My name is Brent. I am a former musician and teacher turned Front End Developer. I build websites and web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I firmly believe everyone should find an occupation they enjoy. Which is why I have had other careers and is why I am transitioning to becoming a developer. For me, a job needs to be something I enjoy doing and something that gives me satisfaction at the end of the day. Programming does that for me. I can look back at the end of the day and see the results of what I have built. I love coding. I love creating and problem solving. I want to learn and grow as a developer!
